6.2 Family Discounts

  • 2nd child: 25% off their enrollment
  • 3rd child and beyond: 50% off each additional child's enrollment

Family discounts apply to the same household and are based on the highest-priced enrollment tier.

6.3 Founders' Rate Lock

The first 10 families to enroll are eligible for a Founders' Rate Lock, which guarantees their initial pricing for as long as their enrollment remains active and in good standing. This offer is limited and non-transferable.

6.4 Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Achievement Academy does not store credit card numbers or sensitive payment data on its servers. By enrolling, you agree to Stripe's terms of service.

6.5 Billing

  • Monthly plans are billed on the same date each month.
  • Annual plans are billed once per year.
  • Package purchases (tutoring hours, summer program full payment, a la carte courses) are billed at the time of purchase.

6.6 Refund Policy

  • Monthly subscriptions: You may cancel at any time. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. No partial-month refunds.
  • Annual subscriptions: If cancelled within the first 30 days, you may receive a prorated refund minus any months used. After 30 days, no refunds are issued, but you retain access through the end of the annual term.
  • Tutoring packages: Unused hours are non-refundable but do not expire for 12 months from date of purchase.
  • Summer program: Refunds are available if requested at least 14 days before the program start date. No refunds after the program begins.
  • A La Carte courses: Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if less than 25% of the course content has been accessed.

6.7 Late or Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we will attempt to notify you and retry the charge. If payment is not resolved within 14 days, the associated account(s) may be suspended until the balance is settled.
